Education Review Panel

 

The Minister's Panel on Education has launched its public consultation phase. Nova Scotians can fill out a survey, submit written comments and call in with comments. There's also a discussion guide. EECD has asked school boards and schools to assist in publicizing the consultation. The link to the consultation page is: www.ednet.ns.ca/educationreview .
